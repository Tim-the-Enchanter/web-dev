<Code Hard!>

Over the past 20 years I have designed, destroyed and rebuilt many websites, programs using so many different tools to make the same result. I am finally to a point where I have found a comfortable easy button for writing code and using GUI based programs to check things out live as I build.

Read More

I built this site using some great support and guidance from the classes I am taking at WSUTech as well as online tools and tutorials. One huge change in the way I do things now is that I am not ashamed or feel like I am cheating by using guides, cheat sheets or templates. The resources we have now, compared to 20 years ago, are immeasurable by today's standards. The sheer abundance of coder and developer communities worldwide make it exciting to dive back into. I have a "toolbox" of code, cheat sheets and prebuilt favorites I have created, so that I can re-create and tweak as I go. I feel like this signature design style I created is mine and the consistency will span my projects as well as grow and evolve as I learn new techniques. I feel like there is so many more options nowadays without having to sweat the "flash" objects, generic tables or "iframes" across multiple devices. I am having fun with this new found drive to create in another medium. Thank you for taking the time to read and share this journey with me! -Tim
